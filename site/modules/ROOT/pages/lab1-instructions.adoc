= Lab 1 Instructions: Neo4j Aura Setup

Follow these steps to sign up for Neo4j Aura and create your database instance.

TIP: For concepts and background, see xref:lab1.adoc[Lab 1: Concepts and Reference].

== Prerequisites

* Completed **Lab 0** (environment setup)
* For **Workshop SSO Login**: Access to OneBlink credentials page (provided by your organizer)
* For **Free Trial Signup**: A valid email address

== Part 1: Neo4j Aura Signup

There are two signup options for this lab. **Follow the signup process provided by your workshop organizer.**

=== Option A: Workshop SSO Login (Recommended for organized workshops)

If your organizer has provided OneBlink credentials, use the SSO login process:

1. Navigate to the OneBlink credentials page to retrieve your SSO login information (Org ID, Email, Password)
+
image::lab1-oneblink-credentials.png[OneBlink credentials page]
2. Go to https://console-preview.neo4j.io/[console-preview.neo4j.io]
3. Click **Continue with Organization SSO**
+
image::lab1-continue-sso.png[Continue with Organization SSO]
4. Enter the **Org ID** from your OneBlink credentials, then click **Continue with OneBlink SSO**
+
image::lab1-oneblink-sso.png[Continue with OneBlink SSO]
5. Enter your **Email** and **Password** from the OneBlink credentials page

=== Option B: Free Trial Signup (For self-paced learning)

If you are completing this lab independently or your organizer has instructed you to create a free trial:

1. Navigate to https://console-preview.neo4j.io/[console-preview.neo4j.io]
2. Click **Don't have an account? Sign up**
3. Follow the sign-up process to create your Neo4j Aura account
4. When prompted, select **AWS** as the cloud provider and **US East (N. Virginia)** as the region
+
image::lab1-free-trial-region.png[Free trial region selection]
5. Click **Start 14-day free trial**
6. **Save your credentials immediately** — click **Download to continue**. The password is only shown once.
+
image::lab1-free-trial-credentials.png[Free trial credentials]

NOTE: If you signed up using the Free Trial option, your instance was already created during signup. Skip ahead to xref:lab1-load.adoc[Load the Knowledge Graph].

=== Create Your Database Instance

1. After logging in, click on **Instances** in the left sidebar under "Data services", then click **Create instance**
+
image::lab1-create-instance.png[Create Instance]
2. Configure your new instance:
+
[cols="1,2"]
|===
| Setting | Value

| **Tier** | Professional
| **Instance name** | A unique name based on your name (e.g., `ryans-lab-instance`)
| **Sizing** | 4 GB RAM / 1 CPU
| **Vector-optimized** | Enabled (under Additional settings)
|===

3. Click **Create** to provision your database instance
+
image::lab1-configure-instance.png[Configure Instance]

=== Save Your Credentials

IMPORTANT: After clicking Create, a credentials dialog appears with your Connection URI, Username, and Password. **Save this information immediately** — the password will not be shown again.

image::lab1-save-credentials.png[Save Credentials]

Click **Download and continue** to save the credentials file. You will need these in:

* Lab 2: Aura Agents configuration
* Labs 3-6: `CONFIG.txt` for notebook configuration
